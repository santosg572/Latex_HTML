\( \mathbf{T} (x, \mathbf{y}; h) = f(x,y) - \frac{1}{h} [\mathbf{u}(x+h) - \mathbf{u}(x) ] \)

\( \mathbf{T} (x, \mathbf{y}; h) = \mathbf{u}'(x) - \frac{1}{h} [\mathbf{u}(x+h) - \mathbf{u}(x)] \)

\( = \mathbf{u}'(x) - \frac{1}{h} [\mathbf{u}(x) + h \mathbf{u}'(x) + \frac{1}{2} h^2 \mathbf{u}''(\xi) - \mathbf{u}(x)] \)
